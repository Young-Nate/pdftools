How to Delete Pages from a PDF

1

Upload your PDF

Click "Select PDF File" or drag and drop your PDF into the upload zone. The file is loaded directly in your browser — nothing is sent to any server.

2

Select pages to delete

Click on any page thumbnail (or its checkbox) to mark it for deletion. Selected pages are highlighted in red. Use "Select All" or "Deselect All" for quick selection.

3

Click "Delete Selected Pages"

Hit the Delete button. The tool will remove every checked page and rebuild the PDF with the remaining pages in original order.

4

Download your PDF

Click "Download PDF" to save the cleaned-up file to your device. The original file is never modified.
