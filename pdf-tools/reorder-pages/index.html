How to Reorder PDF Pages

1

Upload your PDF

Click "Select PDF File" or drag and drop your PDF into the upload zone. Your file is loaded directly in your browser — nothing is sent to any server.

2

Drag pages into order

Each page appears as a thumbnail card. Drag any card to a new position to rearrange it. The red number on each card shows its new position in the final document.

3

Click "Save Reordered PDF"

Hit the button and the tool will rebuild the PDF with pages in the exact order you arranged them, at full original quality.

4

Download your PDF

Click "Download Reordered PDF" to save the file. You can also click "Reset order" at any time to go back to the original page sequence.
