How to Extract Pages from a PDF

1

Upload your PDF

Click "Select PDF File" or drag and drop your PDF into the upload zone. The file is processed entirely in your browser — no data leaves your device.

2

Enter the pages you want

Type the page numbers or ranges you need in the input field. For example: 1, 3, 5-8 will extract pages 1, 3, 5, 6, 7, and 8. A live preview shows exactly which pages will be included.

3

Click "Extract Pages"

Hit the button and the tool will build a new PDF containing only your selected pages, preserving their original quality and order.

4

Download your PDF

Click "Download Extracted PDF" to save the new file to your computer. The original file is never modified.
