How to Compress a PDF

1

Upload your PDF

Drag and drop your PDF or click "Select PDF File". The file stays on your device — nothing is uploaded.

2

Choose compression level

Select Low (best quality), Medium (balanced), or High (smallest file). Medium is recommended for most documents.

3

Click "Compress PDF"

The compression runs in your browser. Larger files may take a few seconds.

4

Download your compressed PDF

See the size reduction and download your optimized PDF with one click.
