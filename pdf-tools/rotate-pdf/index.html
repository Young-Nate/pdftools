How to Rotate PDF Pages

1

Upload your PDF

Click "Select PDF File" or drag your PDF into the upload area. The tool will detect the total number of pages automatically.

2

Select which pages to rotate

Choose "All pages", "Odd pages", "Even pages", or enter specific page numbers and ranges (e.g. 1, 3, 5-8).

3

Choose rotation angle

Select 90° clockwise, 180°, or 90° counter-clockwise depending on how the pages need to be corrected.

4

Download the result

Click "Rotate PDF" and then download your corrected document instantly.
