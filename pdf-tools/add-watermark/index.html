How to Add a Watermark to a PDF

1

Upload your PDF

Click "Select PDF File" or drag and drop your PDF into the upload zone. The file is processed entirely in your browser — no data is sent to any server.

2

Configure your watermark

Enter your watermark text (e.g. DRAFT, CONFIDENTIAL), then adjust the font size, color, opacity, and position. A live preview shows exactly how the watermark will look on the page.

3

Click "Apply Watermark"

The watermark is drawn on every page of your PDF using your chosen settings. The diagonal center position automatically rotates the text 45° for a classic watermark look.

4

Download your PDF

Click "Download Watermarked PDF" to save the result. The original file is never modified.
