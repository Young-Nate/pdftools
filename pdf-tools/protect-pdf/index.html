How to Password-Protect a PDF

1

Upload your PDF

Click "Select PDF File" or drag and drop your PDF into the upload zone. Your file is loaded directly in your browser — nothing is uploaded to any server.

2

Set your passwords

Enter an owner password (required) to control permissions. Optionally add a user password that recipients must enter to open the document.

3

Choose permissions

Select which operations to allow or restrict: printing, copying text, modifying content, adding annotations, filling forms, or assembling pages.

4

Download your protected PDF

Click "Protect PDF" and download the secured file. Share it knowing it has the restrictions you configured.
