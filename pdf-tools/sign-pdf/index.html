How to Sign a PDF Online

1

Upload your PDF

Click "Select PDF File" or drag and drop your document. Your file stays in your browser — no uploading to any server.

2

Create your signature

Draw your signature freely with a mouse or touch, or type your name and choose a handwriting-style font. Adjust the ink color and pen size for drawn signatures.

3

Place the signature

Click anywhere on the page preview to position your signature exactly where you want it. Use the size slider to resize it.

4

Download your signed PDF

Click "Apply Signature & Download" — your signature is embedded directly into the PDF as a permanent image, ready to share.

Frequently Asked Questions

This tool creates an electronic signature — an image of your signature embedded into the PDF. It is not a cryptographic digital signature (which requires a certificate authority). For most everyday documents, contracts, and forms, an electronic signature is legally valid in many jurisdictions including the EU (eIDAS) and US (ESIGN Act).
