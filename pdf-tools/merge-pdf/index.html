How to Merge PDF Files

1

Upload your PDF files

Click "Select PDF Files" or drag and drop multiple PDFs into the upload zone. You can add as many files as you need.

2

Reorder if needed

Drag and drop the files in your preferred order. The first file listed will become the first pages of your merged document.

3

Click "Merge PDFs"

Hit the Merge button and wait a moment. Your browser will process everything locally — no files leave your device.

4

Download your merged PDF

Once done, click "Download Merged PDF" to save the combined file to your computer.
