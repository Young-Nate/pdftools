How to Split a PDF

1

Upload your PDF

Click "Select PDF File" or drag and drop your document into the upload zone. Only one PDF file is needed.

2

Choose a split mode

Select "Split all pages" to extract every page individually, or choose "Custom ranges" to define your own groups (e.g. 1-3, 4-6, 7).

3

Click "Split PDF"

Your browser processes the PDF locally and creates all output files instantly. No server needed.

4

Download your files

If you split into multiple files, they download as a convenient ZIP archive. A single output file downloads directly.
