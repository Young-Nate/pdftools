How to Edit a PDF Online

1

Upload your PDF

Click "Select PDF File" or drag and drop your document. Your file is loaded directly in your browser — nothing leaves your device.

2

Choose your annotation tool

Select "Add Text" to place text boxes anywhere on the page. Select "Highlight" to drag and draw colored highlight rectangles over content.

3

Add your annotations

Click on the page to add text, type your content, and adjust font, size, and color. Drag to create highlight boxes in your chosen color. Navigate pages to annotate the whole document.

4

Save and download

Click "Save & Download PDF" to burn all annotations into the PDF permanently and download your edited file.
