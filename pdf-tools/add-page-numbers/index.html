How to Add Page Numbers to a PDF

1

Upload your PDF

Click "Select PDF File" or drag your document into the upload zone. The tool reads the total page count automatically.

2

Configure your options

Choose the position (top or bottom, left / center / right), start number, font size, and an optional prefix like "Page " or "- ".

3

Preview and confirm

Check the live preview strip to see exactly what your page number will look like before applying it.

4

Download the result

Click "Add Page Numbers" and download your stamped PDF in seconds.
